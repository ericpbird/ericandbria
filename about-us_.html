Our story isn’t just about dance; it’s about finding each other through dance and growing together, step by step. We are Eric and Bria Bird, and our paths first intertwined on the dance floor at Brigham Young University in 2008. It wasn’t just our steps that were in sync; there was an undeniable connection that went beyond dance.

As a couple, we began our competitive journey in novice standard, evolving to the elegance of amateur smooth and international ballroom. Our dance journey mirrored our life together – intricate, challenging, yet immensely rewarding. We celebrated our love by getting married in 2009, and by 2015, we took our partnership to a professional level in American Smooth, a style that resonates deeply with us both.

Dance, for us, has been more than a series of movements; it’s been our shared language of love and understanding. The lessons we’ve learned on the dance floor in partnership, communication, and resolving conflicts have been pivotal in our marriage. We’ve discovered that the harmony needed for a dance routine is akin to the harmony in a successful marriage.

Our life together is a dance – a blend of rhythm, passion, and mutual respect – both on and off the dance floor. We believe that our journey as dancers and as partners showcases the transformative power of dance in building and strengthening relationships.

Come, share in our dance of life, where every step we’ve taken together tells a story of love, learning, and togetherness.

I’m Bria Bird, a passionate dancer and dedicated educator. My dance journey started in high school and grew profoundly at Brigham Young University, where I earned a minor in ballroom dancing alongside my BS in Microbiology. Dance was my respite from the rigors of academic life, aiding in balancing my mind and spirit.

At BYU, I engaged deeply in dance, honing my skills and embracing its transformative power. As a teacher of advanced placement classes by day, I bring the same level of commitment and fervor to the dance studio. My experience in dance has been instrumental in managing the demands of a challenging academic career and in my personal development.

I believe in the unique ability of dance to enhance well-being, facilitate communication, and foster connection. As a dance instructor, I am dedicated to sharing this art form, helping others discover the joy and balance it can bring to their lives.

Hello, I’m Eric Bird. My journey with dance began in the vibrant culture of Mexico and was academically honed through earning a minor in ballroom dancing at Brigham Young University. This journey has taught me a profound truth: dance is a powerful agent of transformation.

Starting with salsa and bachata, I discovered a world where rhythm and expression intertwine, deepening my understanding and passion for dance. My exploration spanned various styles, from lively swing to elegant ballroom, each enhancing my love for this art form.

Since 2010, my focus has been on teaching dance. My approach transcends traditional steps and techniques; it’s about empowering individuals, creating moments of joy, and nurturing a sense of community. Whether guiding a novice or refining an experienced dancer’s skill, my goal is to facilitate growth, connection, and celebration through dance.

Join me on this exciting journey, where each step is not just a dance move, but a stride towards personal growth and shared happiness.
